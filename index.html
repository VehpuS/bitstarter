What is Plan A

Plan A is an idea for a service that will allow people to plan events in a more natural way.
  • Plan A will allow you to create events without defining dates - planning starts before that!
  • Plan A will let you host discussions and votes regarding the nature of your events - whether with collaborators or fans.
  • Plan A will help you chose the details of your event, from the venue to the gift you're going to buy, helping you plan ahead for it's price.
  • Plan A will help you invite your events' participants from a variety of sources (using API's) and raise the funds forthose who plan to attend.
